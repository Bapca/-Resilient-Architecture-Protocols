# PDS-GVAP: Personal Digital Sovereignty, Verifiable Presence, and the Edge-AI Guardian

## Abstract
As smartphones and AR devices become direct extensions of human cognition, users face three compounding threats: the rise of AI botnets spoofing human identity, physical street-level coercion (unwarranted searches, forced biometric unlocks), and sophisticated social engineering leading to user-initiated self-harm. We propose a unified architecture combining GVAP (Global Verification of Active Presence) and PDS (Personal Digital Sovereignty), anchored by an offline Edge-AI Guardian that protects the user both from external threats and their own cognitively compromised actions.

## 1. The GVAP Metric: Proof of Humanity & Social Graph
To participate in high-stakes digital environments (corporate access, professional esports), verification must rely on an unforgeable timeline of activity to prevent Sybil attacks by Generative AI.
* **Time-Locked Footprint:** A 1-to-3 year history of Git commits, verified streaming, or community contributions. AI can generate a fake video today, but cannot retroactively fake years of interactive public presence.
* **Meritocratic Access:** This ensures global platforms remain populated by verified, productive humans with an audited history of competence.

## 2. Hardware-Level Anti-Coercion & Compartmentalization
Biometrics (FaceID, Fingerprint) are usernames, not passwords. They are vulnerable to physical force or malicious actors scanning a user's face against their will.
* **Mandatory PIN Fallback:** Systems must feature an instant "Lockdown Mode" disabling all biometrics, requiring a complex 6-8 alphanumeric PIN or pattern.
* **Deniable Compartmentalization:** Apps and local data must utilize hidden encrypted volumes. The presence of specific software on a device must be cryptographically deniable to protect users traversing regions with arbitrary software bans.
* **Multi-Party Social Recovery:** Password resets must require a time-delay and verification (via secure video link) from a pre-established "Trusted Circle," rendering stolen or physically seized devices useless to attackers.

## 3. The Edge-AI Guardian: Proactive & Legal Defense
When a device is seized or the user is manipulated, the last line of defense is a Local AI (Edge AI) running offline on the device's Neural Processing Unit (NPU).
* **The Legal Firewall:** If an unauthorized entity attempts to mirror the device or force access, the Offline AI prompts for a cryptographic digital warrant. Without it, the AI locks the device, wipes volatile memory, and logs the coercive attempt.
* **Behavioral Lockdown:** If local sensors detect stress patterns indicative of physical robbery, the AI activates a decoy environment (dummy apps and balances).
* **Proactive Cognitive Defense (Self-Protection):** The AI acts as the user's personal counsel. It analyzes the user's intended actions in real-time. If the user attempts to execute an impulsive, dangerous, or legally compromising action (e.g., transferring funds to a known scam pattern, sharing sensitive data under emotional duress, or violating safety protocols), the AI intercepts the action. It requires a "cooling-off" period or secondary verification, effectively protecting the user from socially engineered self-harm.

## 4. AR Integration: Ambient Security
Augmented Reality (AR) combined with the AI Guardian provides "Digital Armor" in the physical world.
* **Real-Time Threat Assessment:** AR glasses connected to the local AI analyze the environment. If approached by malicious actors claiming to conduct a "security check," the AR system provides legal context, warns of manipulative tactics, and records the interaction to a hidden partition.
* **Action Contextualization:** In daily life, the AR AI highlights physical hazards and flags deceptive real-world interactions, ensuring the user's physical safety is monitored as rigorously as their digital data.

## Conclusion
The PDS-GVAP architecture transforms the user's device into an autonomous fortress. By enforcing chronological proof of humanity, establishing strict physical anti-coercion locks, and deploying an Edge-AI that defends against both state-level overreach and user-level mistakes, we establish an unbreakable baseline of personal sovereignty.
